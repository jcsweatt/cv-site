About Me.

Associate Registrar & Systems Leader

I serve as Associate Registrar at the University of California, San Francisco Opens in a new window, working at the intersection of academic policy, systems, and operations. My focus is simple: take complex institutional processes and make them clear, functional, and sustainable.

I am a primary resource for academic policy interpretation and development, partnering across the institution to ensure consistency, compliance, and operational alignment. Much of my work sits where governance, systems, and real-world execution meet, especially in moments where those pieces do not quite line up.

I have led major systems initiatives, including the implementation of a graduate admissions platform and ongoing work supporting UCSF's transition to Oracle Cloud. I approach this work with a strong emphasis on usability, scalability, and long-term maintainability.

Originally from Germany and now based in the Bay Area, I bring both precision and practicality to building processes that support a modern academic environment.

My approach is direct: if a process is confusing, it is not finished.
